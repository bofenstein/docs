2du gives you access to more than 500,000 local business listings searchable right on the map. Whether you know exactly what you're looking for or you're browsing by type, you can find restaurants, retailers, services, and specialty shops for any location in the world — and go from search result to directions in just a few taps.

## Search methods

You have four ways to find businesses on the 2du map:

- **By category** — browse businesses grouped by type, such as restaurants, hotels, pharmacies, gyms, or shops. Useful when you want to see all options in a given area without a specific business in mind.
- **By name** — search for a specific business by its exact or partial name. Ideal when you already know where you want to go.
- **By keyword** — enter any descriptive term to find businesses that match. Search "coffee beans," "yoga classes," or "vintage records" to surface niche results that standard maps often miss.
- **By brand** — find local retailers that carry a specific brand. Search "Stella McCartney," for example, to locate nearby stores that stock her line.

<Tip>
  Use keyword search to uncover specialty products or niche services that don't appear in typical category-based map searches. It's especially useful for finding independent boutiques, specialty food shops, and service providers.
</Tip>

## How to search on the map

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open 2du and go to the map view">
    Launch the app and navigate to the map. Your current location loads by default.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move the map to your target location">
    Drag the map to the area you want to search. You can search anywhere in the world, not just where you are right now.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap the search icon">
    Tap the search icon on the map screen to open the search bar.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Enter your search term">
    Type a category (e.g., "restaurants"), a business name, a keyword (e.g., "organic bakery"), or a brand name (e.g., "Stella McCartney"). 2du searches across all 500,000+ listings.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse results as map pins">
    Matching businesses appear as pins on the map. Scan the area visually to get a sense of what's nearby and how businesses are distributed.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Tap a pin to see business details">
    Tap any pin to open the business detail view — name, address, category, hours, and more.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Get directions or preview the location">
    From the detail view, tap **Directions** for turn-by-turn navigation, or tap **Street View** for an immersive 360° preview of the location before you visit.
  </Step>
</Steps>
