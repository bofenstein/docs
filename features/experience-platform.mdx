The 2du Experience Platform puts the 200 closest websites and digital resources for any location on earth directly in your hands. Whether you're standing on a street corner in Tokyo, planning a trip to Lisbon from your living room, or dreaming about a destination you've never visited, the platform surfaces the most relevant content for that exact spot — automatically, in real time.

## Where the content comes from

2du aggregates location-tagged content from hundreds of sources so you always have a rich, multi-dimensional view of any place. Sources include:

- **Video & social** — YouTube, Facebook, Twitter
- **Travel booking** — Orbitz, TripAdvisor, Airbnb, Booking.com, Viator
- **Search & discovery** — Google, Yelp
- **Editorial & expert content** — travel bloggers, local TV, radio, and news outlets
- **Tour & hospitality** — tour operators, cruise lines, tourist bureaus
- **Education & culture** — universities, historical event archives
- **Local commerce** — brick-and-mortar businesses in the area

<Note>
  Every piece of content on the Experience Platform is 100% location-based. What you see is determined entirely by where the map cursor is pointing — not by ads, algorithms, or promoted placements.
</Note>

## Categories of content

When you explore a location, content is organized into intuitive categories:

- **News & Entertainment** — local headlines, video clips, and social posts tied to that area
- **Travel Bookings** — hotels, flights, tours, and experiences you can reserve directly
- **Local Experiences** — restaurants, attractions, events, and hidden gems curated by locals
- **Travel Bloggers** — personal perspectives and itineraries from creators who've been there
- **Tour Operators** — guided experiences, day trips, and curated adventures available at that location

## How to explore

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open the 2du app">
    Launch 2du on your device. The Experience Platform loads automatically and surfaces content for your current location.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Browse content for where you are">
    Scroll through the content feed to see videos, blogs, bookings, and local business info relevant to your current position.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Move the map cursor to explore anywhere">
    Drag the map to any location on earth — a city you're planning to visit, a neighborhood you're curious about, or a landmark you've always wanted to see. The content feed updates instantly for wherever the cursor lands.
  </Step>
</Steps>
