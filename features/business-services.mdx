2du offers a full suite of business membership services designed to help local brands connect with customers who are physically nearby or actively exploring your area. From a hyper-local profile page to proximity-triggered loyalty programs, 2du puts your business in front of the right people at exactly the right moment.

## Services available to member businesses

### BizDetails landing pages

Create a dedicated, hyper-local profile page for your business that's visible to 2du users browsing your area. Your BizDetails page can include your address, hours, contact information, product or service highlights, and links to your website — giving nearby users everything they need to walk through your door or place an order.

### LIVE customer support

Connect with customers in real time directly through the 2du app. When a user is browsing near your location and has a question, your team can respond immediately — turning passive interest into active engagement before the moment passes.

### Video demonstrations

Showcase your products, services, or experience through video. Upload demonstration clips that play directly on your business listing, giving potential customers a compelling preview before they visit.

### Map visibility enhancements

Increase your prominence on the 2du map so your business stands out when users are searching or browsing in your area. Enhanced visibility helps you capture attention at the exact moment someone is looking for what you offer.

### Proximity-triggered loyalty programs

Automatically reward customers when they're near your location. Proximity-triggered programs fire based on a user's physical proximity to your business — encouraging repeat visits and building loyalty without requiring manual check-ins or app interactions from the customer.

### Email campaigns

Send targeted emails to 2du users who've shown interest in your area or category. Reach people who are already engaged with content near your location and bring them to your door with timely, relevant offers.

<Tip>
  2du is designed to complement businesses that already have delivery and e-commerce channels. Use your BizDetails page and in-app listing to drive traffic to your existing website, ordering system, or storefront — not to replace them.
</Tip>

<Note>
  These services are available to registered 2du business members. Visit the [For Businesses](/for-businesses) page to learn how to get listed, view pricing, and sign up.
</Note>
