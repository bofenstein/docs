As you explore any location in 2du, the in-app advertising section surfaces thousands of products, services, and experiences that are directly relevant to where you're looking. From local hotels to concert tickets to merchandise from your favorite sports team, 2du connects you to purchase opportunities specific to each place — without leaving the app.

## What you can find and buy

The in-app purchasing section covers a broad range of location-based commerce:

- **Local hotels and accommodations** — browse and book stays near wherever you're exploring
- **Local sports team merchandise** — find official gear for the home team in any city
- **Local auto dealers** — explore vehicle inventory at dealerships in the area
- **Real estate listings** — view properties for sale or rent in any neighborhood
- **Concert and event tickets** — discover and purchase tickets for shows and events nearby
- **Tourist attractions** — buy entry tickets to museums, landmarks, and theme parks
- **Local tour operators** — book guided tours, day trips, and curated experiences
- **E-commerce from 100+ well-known brands** — shop from major retailers and brands with a local presence

## How it works

As you move the map cursor to any location, 2du's in-app advertising section automatically updates to show deals, promotions, and purchasing opportunities specific to that place. You don't need to search — relevant offers surface on their own based on where you're browsing. Scroll through the section to discover what's available, tap any listing to learn more, and complete your purchase through the merchant's own website.

<Note>
  2du is designed to complement brands that already have delivery and e-commerce channels, not replace them. Tapping any listing opens the merchant's own website, where you complete the transaction on their platform.
</Note>
