The 2du team is available for a range of inquiries — whether you need help with the app, want to explore a business partnership, or are working on a press story. Use the contact details below to reach the right team directly.

<Note>
  Contact information shown is for reference; visit [2du.com/contact](https://2du.com/contact) for current details.
</Note>

## Get in touch

<CardGroup cols={2}>
  <Card title="General inquiries" icon="envelope">
    For general questions about 2du, send an email to [support@2du.com](mailto:hello@yourappsite.com). This is the best place to start if you're not sure who to contact.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Technical support" icon="circle-question">
    Experiencing an issue with the app? Email [support@2du.com](mailto:support@yourappsite.com). The support team handles bug reports, account problems, and anything not working as expected.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Press and media" icon="newspaper">
    For press inquiries, interview requests, or media assets, contact [support@2du.com](mailto:press@yourappsite.com). Please include your publication name and deadline in your message.
  </Card>

  <Card title="Partnerships" icon="handshake">
    Interested in partnering with 2du? Reach the partnerships team at [support@2du.com](mailto:partners@yourappsite.com) to discuss integration opportunities, co-marketing, or business development.
  </Card>
</CardGroup>

<Note>
  Support hours for the technical support team are 9am–5pm Monday to Friday (GMT). Paid plan members receive 24/7 email support outside these hours.
</Note>

## Before you contact us

<Tip>
  Many common questions are already answered in the [FAQ](/faq). Check there first — you may find an instant answer without waiting for a reply.
</Tip>

When you do reach out to support, include as much detail as possible: your device model, operating system version, a description of the issue, and any steps you've already tried. This helps the team resolve your issue faster.
