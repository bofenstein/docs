2du offers a free plan so you can start exploring local experiences right away. If you want more storage or are a heavy user, paid plans give you everything in the free tier plus significantly more capacity — with no setup fees and the flexibility to change plans whenever your needs shift.

## Compare plans

| Feature | Basic | Plus | Premium |
|---|---|---|---|
| **Monthly price** | Free | $29.99/mo | $69.99/mo |
| **Storage** | 2 GB | 100 GB | 500 GB |
| Free updates | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Advanced options | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| Email support | Yes | Yes | Yes |
| 24/7 support | Yes | Yes | Yes |

<Note>
  The **Plus plan** is the recommended choice for most users. At $29.99/month, it gives you 100 GB of storage — enough for active use — while keeping costs predictable.
</Note>

<Tip>
  You can upgrade, downgrade, or cancel your plan at any time from your account settings. Changes take effect at the start of your next billing cycle.
</Tip>

## Sign up for 2du

You can create a free 2du account in seconds using any of the following options:

- **Twitter** — Sign in with your existing Twitter account.
- **Facebook** — Sign in with your existing Facebook account.
- **Google** — Sign in with your existing Google account.
- **Email** — Register with an email address and password of your choice.

All sign-up methods give you immediate access to the free Basic plan. You can upgrade to Plus or Premium at any time from your account settings.

## Business pricing

The plans above are designed for individual users. If you represent a restaurant, hotel, retail shop, or other local business, 2du offers a separate set of App-as-a-Service features tailored to your needs.

See [For Businesses](/for-businesses) for details on BizDetails pages, map visibility, loyalty programs, and more.
