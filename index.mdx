## What you can do with 2du

2du brings together content from YouTube, TripAdvisor, Yelp, Airbnb, Booking.com, Viator, local news outlets, travel bloggers, and 100+ other platforms — all organized by location. Open the app, and you instantly see what's happening, what's worth visiting, and where to stay or eat — all within reach of where you are (or anywhere you want to explore).

<Steps>
  <Step title="Download 2du">
    Get the app from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store. It's free to download and use.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow location access">
    2du uses your location to show you the most relevant local content. You can also manually move the map to explore any location in the world.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Explore your surroundings">
    Browse the 200 closest websites and resources tied to your current location — from travel videos and local news to hotel bookings and restaurant reviews.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Search for businesses">
    Use the built-in map to find local businesses by name, category, keyword, or even the brands they carry — with one-tap directions.
  </Step>
</Steps>
