2du is free to download on iPhone and Android. Once installed, all you need to do is allow location access and the app immediately starts showing you local content for wherever you are. The whole setup takes under two minutes.

## Install 2du on your device

<Steps>
  <Step title="Open your device's app store">
    On iPhone, open the **App Store**. On Android, open the **Google Play Store**.
  </Step>
  <Step title='Search for "2du"'>
    Tap the search bar and type **2du**. Look for the official 2du app in the results.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Download and install">
    Tap **Get** (iPhone) or **Install** (Android). The app downloads and installs automatically.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Allow location permissions">
    When you open 2du for the first time, the app asks for location access. Tap **Allow** to let the app show you content relevant to where you are.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start exploring">
    Your local feed loads immediately. You'll see the 200 closest websites, videos, reviews, and resources tied to your current location — ready to browse.
  </Step>
</Steps>

<Note>
  2du needs location access to show you relevant nearby content. If you prefer not to share your location, you can still use the app — just drag the map manually to any location in the world to browse its local content.
</Note>

<Tip>
  Visit [2du.com](https://2du.com) on your phone and scan the QR code to go directly to the correct download page for your device. No searching required.
</Tip>
