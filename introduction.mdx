2du is a location-based everything app that shows you the world through the lens of wherever you are standing — or wherever you want to explore. Open the app and you immediately see the 200 closest websites that define your current location: travel videos, local news, hotel and restaurant bookings, experience guides, and more. Move the map to any city or country in the world, and the entire content feed updates to reflect that new location.

## How 2du works

When you open 2du, the app detects your location and instantly surfaces the 200 closest websites tied to that place. A result could be a Facebook page for the shop in front of you, a YouTube travel video reviewing the restaurants on your street, a TripAdvisor listing for a nearby attraction, or a local news segment about an event happening this weekend.

Every piece of content is anchored to a real geographic location — not an algorithm's guess at what you might like. The app is 100% location-based.

You are not limited to where you physically are. Drag the map cursor to any location worldwide and explore a brand-new local experience: browse hotels in Tokyo, read restaurant reviews in Rome, or watch travel videos about a national park you're planning to visit — all without leaving the app.

## Platforms 2du brings together

2du aggregates content from a wide range of sources so you never have to switch between apps:

- **Video & travel content** — YouTube, travel bloggers, tour operators
- **Reviews & recommendations** — TripAdvisor, Yelp, Google
- **Accommodation & experiences** — Airbnb, Booking.com, Viator
- **Local news & media** — local TV stations, radio stations, and news outlets

## Who uses 2du

2du is built for two kinds of explorers:

- **Travelers** who want to research a destination before they arrive, find things to do on the ground, and book experiences without juggling multiple apps.
- **Locals** who want to discover what's happening in their own community — a new restaurant that opened nearby, a local news story, or an upcoming event.

Whether you're planning a trip or just curious about what's around the corner, 2du gives you a complete picture of any location on earth.
