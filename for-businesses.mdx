2du offers App-as-a-Service features to local and regional businesses that want to reach customers who are actively nearby. Whether you run a single storefront or manage multiple locations, 2du puts your business in front of people already exploring your area — at exactly the moment they're looking for what you offer.

## Why list on 2du

2du places your business directly in the path of nearby explorers. Users open the app to discover what's around them — restaurants, shops, experiences, and services — and they browse by location as they move through the map. When your business is listed, you appear naturally in that discovery flow without competing for attention through ads or search rankings alone.

Customers who find you through 2du are already close by and already interested. That means higher intent and a more direct path from discovery to visit.

- Local retailers and boutiques

If your business benefits from foot traffic, local discovery, or proximity-based decisions, 2du is built for you.

## Getting started

<Steps>
  <Step title="Contact 2du to express interest">
    Reach out through the [contact page](/contact) to let the team know you want to list your business. A 2du representative will follow up to walk you through onboarding.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Set up your BizDetails profile">
    Work with the 2du team to build your BizDetails page. Add your business name, address, hours, photos, and any other details you want nearby customers to see.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Configure your map presence and loyalty program">
    Confirm your map pin location and set up your loyalty program rules — how customers earn rewards, what those rewards are, and how they redeem them.
  </Step>
  <Step title="Start reaching nearby customers">
    Once your profile is live, you appear on the 2du map and in local discovery results. Launch your first email campaign to start driving visits.
  </Step>
</Steps>
