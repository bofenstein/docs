This page covers the questions 2du users ask most often — from getting started and understanding the app's features to managing your plan and troubleshooting common issues. If you can't find what you're looking for here, visit the [Contact page](/contact) to reach the support team.

## General questions

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What is 2du?">
    2du is a location-based experience platform available on iOS and Android. It shows you the 200 closest websites for any location — including travel videos, local news, hotel bookings, restaurant reviews, and business listings. You can explore your current location or move the map to discover any place in the world.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Is 2du free to use?">
    Yes, 2du has a free Basic plan that lets you explore local content, search for businesses, and use the map. Paid plans (Plus at $29.99/month and Premium at $69.99/month) offer additional storage and features for more demanding users.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What devices does 2du work on?">
    2du is available for iPhone (iOS) and Android smartphones. Download it from the Apple App Store or Google Play Store.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Features

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How does the location-based content work?">
    When you open 2du, it uses your device's GPS to identify your location. It then shows you the 200 closest websites and resources relevant to that spot — from travel blogs and YouTube videos to TripAdvisor reviews and local business pages. Move the map cursor to any location worldwide and the content updates instantly.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What kinds of content will I see?">
    2du aggregates content from YouTube, TripAdvisor, Yelp, Airbnb, Booking.com, Viator, local news and radio stations, travel bloggers, tour operators, and 100+ other platforms. You'll see a mix of travel videos, local news, hotel and experience bookings, restaurant reviews, and business listings — all tied to the location you're viewing.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I search for local businesses?">
    Tap the search icon on the map and enter a category (e.g., "restaurants"), a business name, a keyword (e.g., "coffee beans"), or a brand name (e.g., "Stella McCartney"). Results appear as map pins. Tap any pin for business details, one-tap street view, or turn-by-turn directions.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I save my favorite places?">
    Yes, you can save businesses and locations as favorites so you can quickly return to them in the future. Access your saved places from your account profile at any time.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Pricing

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="What's included in the free plan?">
    The free Basic plan includes 2 GB of storage, full access to all location-based content, local business search, and email support with 24/7 availability. It's a complete experience for casual exploration with no credit card required.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="Can I upgrade or downgrade my plan?">
    Yes, you can change your plan at any time from within your account settings. Upgrades take effect immediately; downgrades apply at the start of your next billing cycle.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I sign up?">
    You can create a 2du account using your Twitter, Facebook, or Google account, or by registering with an email address and password. Tap **Sign up** on the app's welcome screen to get started.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Business accounts

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="How do I list my business on 2du?">
    Contact the 2du team to get started. Once you become a member, you can set up a BizDetails landing page, enhance your map presence, and activate loyalty programs and email campaigns for nearby users. See the [For Businesses](/for-businesses) page for details.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="What is a BizDetails page?">
    A BizDetails page is a hyper-local profile page for your business that appears to 2du users who are nearby. It can include your business details, video demos, customer support chat, and links to your website or e-commerce store — giving you a direct channel to customers at the moment they're closest to you.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>

## Troubleshooting

<AccordionGroup>
  <Accordion title="Why isn't the app showing content for my location?">
    Make sure location permissions are enabled for 2du in your phone's settings. Go to **Settings > Privacy > Location Services**, find 2du, and set it to **While Using the App** or **Always**. If the problem persists, try restarting the app.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="The map isn't loading — what should I do?">
    Check your internet connection. 2du requires an active data connection to load map content. If your connection is stable and the map still won't load, try closing and reopening the app or clearing the app cache from your device settings.
  </Accordion>

  <Accordion title="How do I contact support?">
    Visit the [Contact page](/contact) to reach the 2du support team. Support is available by email with 24/7 service for paid plan members and business hours support for free plan users.
  </Accordion>
</AccordionGroup>
